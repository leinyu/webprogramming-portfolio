Spider-Man: No Way Home

The Plot

After Mysterio reveals Peter Parker's identity, Peter's life is turned upside down. He asks Doctor Strange to cast a spell to make the world forget, but the magic goes wrong, pulling villains from across the multiverse into his world. Peter must now catch these villains and send them back, while facing the toughest choice of his life.
