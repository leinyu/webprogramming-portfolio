Classic Smashed Avocado Toast

A simple, healthy, and undeniably iconic breakfast. This recipe features creamy avocado on toasted sourdough.

Ingredients

  • 1 thick slice of sourdough
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Method

  1. Toast the sourdough bread until it is golden.
  2. Smash the avocado with lemon juice and salt.
  3. Spread the mixture and serve!
