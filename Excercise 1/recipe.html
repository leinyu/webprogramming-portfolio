Classic Avocado Toast

A simple, nutritious, and creamy breakfast staple that takes less than 5 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • A squeeze of fresh lemon juice

Method

  1. Toast the sourdough slices until golden brown.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with the lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture evenly across the toast.
  4. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and serve immediately.
