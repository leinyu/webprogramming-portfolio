Classic Smashed Avocado Toast

A nutritious, creamy, and crunchy breakfast staple that takes less than 10 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 Ripe avocado
  • 2 Slices of sourdough or whole-grain bread
  • 1 Teaspoon of lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • A drizzle of extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. Toast your bread slices until they are golden brown and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a small bowl.
  3. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the bowl and mash with a fork until desired consistency is reached.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture generously over the warm toast.
  5. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and a light drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Serve immediately while the bread is still crunchy.
