The Story

Immediately following the events of Far From Home, Peter Parker's life is turned upside down when Mysterio reveals his identity to the world. Seeking to regain his anonymity, Peter turns to Doctor Stephen Strange for a mystical solution.

However, the spell is corrupted, causing the barriers between universes to shatter. Peter must capture villains from alternative realities—including Doctor Octopus and the Green Goblin—before they can destroy his world. The stakes become deeply personal as Peter learns that "with great power, there must also come great responsibility."
